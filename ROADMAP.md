# php-jobspy Roadmap

This package is designed to be a standalone, deployable library for aggregating job postings across multiple platforms cleanly into structured local data.

## Vision and Capabilities
1. **Inspired by `python-jobspy`**: Emulate the clean, dependency-light, and robust approach of its Python counterpart to pull raw postings directly from targeted platforms without middle-man web overhead.
2. **Universal Job Board Scraping**: Capabilities to scrape and seamlessly adopt the scraping mechanics of major job boards (LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, etc.).
3. **Niche & Targeted Web Careers**: Capabilities to scrape specific web careers available in a targeted geographic area or professional niche (e.g., Senior PHP Developers in the Netherlands).
4. **Intelligent Generation**: Generate a structured list of applicable jobs (arrays/objects) based on granular search criteria to feed downstream pipelines.
